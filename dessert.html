Chocolate Waffle

Ingredient

  1. 1 1⁄3 cups flour
  2. 4 teaspoons baking powder
  3. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. 2 tablespoons sugar
  5. 2 eggs, separated
  6. 1/2 cup butter, melted
  7. 1 3/4cups milk

Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together all dry ingredients.
  2. Separate the eggs, adding the yolks to the dry ingredient mixture, and placing the whites in a small mixing bowl.
  3. Beat whites until moderately stiff; set aside.
  4. Add milk and melted butter to dry ingredient mixture and blend.
  5. Fold stiff egg whites into mixture.
  6. Ladle mixture into hot waffle iron and bake.

Bread Pudding Pie

Ingredient

  1. Dough for single-crust pie
  2. 1 cup cubed bread
  3. 2 large eggs
  4. 2 cups 2% milk
  5. 3/4 cup sugar
  6. 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg and 2 teaspoons butter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°. On a lightly floured surface, roll dough to a 1/8-in.-thick circle; transfer to a 9-in. pie plate. Baklava Thumbprint

Ingredient

  1. 1 cup sugar and 1/2 teaspoon salt
  2. 1/2 cup butter, softened
  3. 2 large eggs, room temperature
  4. 1 teaspoon almond extract
  5. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. 2-1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  7. 1 teaspoon baking powder

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, cream sugar and butter until blended. Beat in eggs, 1 at a time, and extracts. In another bowl, whisk flour, baking powder and salt; gradually beat into creamed mixture.
  2. Wrap dough; refrigerate until firm enough to form into balls, about 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 375°. For topping, combine sugar and cinnamon; set aside. Shape dough into 1-in. balls; refrigerate again if dough becomes too warm.
  4. Place balls 2-1/2 in. apart on parchment-lined baking sheets. Bake 8 minutes.
  5. Press a deep indentation in center of each cookie with the back of a rounded teaspoon. Fill each indentation with honey and walnuts; sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.
  6. Return to oven and bake until edges begin to brown, 7-9 minutes longer. Cool on pans 1 minute. Remove to wire racks to cool. Store in an airtight container.
